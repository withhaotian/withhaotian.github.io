Short Bio

Currently, I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate of Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University (NJU), under the supervision of Prof. Wanchun Dou. From August 2024 to November 2024, I have worked as a Visiting Ph.D. Student at School of Computing, Macquarie University, supervised by Dr. Xuyun Zhang. Before that, I received my M.Sc. in Software Engineering and B.Eng. in Computer Science from Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology (NUIST) in 2023 and 2020, respectively, advised by Prof. Xiaolong Xu.

My research interest is centered on Edge AI Computing/System (a paper list is available in this [repo]), with particular emphasis on distributed edge inference, serverless edge computing, and resource management in cloud-edge computing systems. Involving the exploration of algorithms and systems co-design tailored for Function as a Service (FaaS) applications, especially for ML serving, in cloud-edge-end computing.
